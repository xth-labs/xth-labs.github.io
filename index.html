Principles

What XThereon stands for

Technology changes. Headlines change. Principles should not. XThereon is anchored on a small set of ideas that guide every decision.

01 • SOVEREIGNTY

Your keys, your decisions

XTH is built around self-custody. If you hold the keys, you hold the coins — no accounts to freeze, no gatekeepers to ask for permission.

02 • VERIFIABILITY

Don’t trust — verify

Anyone can run a node, inspect the source, and confirm the supply. The rules are public, the code is open, and the chain is auditable end to end.

03 • ACCESSIBILITY

Start small, start early

XThereon is tuned so that everyday people can participate from day one — not just warehouses of specialised gear. Over time, the network finds its own equilibrium.

FAQ

Straight answers, no marketing fog.

XThereon is not a promise of future riches. It is a tool. Tools are only as good as the people who use them. Here are the questions people with their eyes open tend to ask first.

Is XThereon an investment?

XTH is not offered as an investment product and does not come with guarantees, yields, or promises of profit. It is simply a digital bearer asset — a way to store and transmit value on a public, permissionless ledger.

Who controls XThereon?

No single company, fund, or government controls the network. Consensus rules are enforced by the software run by independent participants. Changes require broad alignment across the community.

What happens if traditional systems wobble?

There are no guarantees in any system. The intention behind XThereon is that, even under stress, anyone with a node, connectivity, and willingness to trade can still agree on who owns what.

Can anyone join?

Yes. If you can download the software and connect to peers, you can hold, send, and mine XTH. No sign-ups, no “accredited investor” forms, no whitelist.
