Built for Independent Thinkers

XThereon (XTH) is a digital currency designed for resilience. When traditional systems fail, XTH remains accessible, unstoppable, and in your control. No pre-mine. No insiders. No middlemen. Just a network that exists to be used.

XTH enters circulation through mining and is exchanged by those who choose to use it.

Technology That Protects You

XThereon is engineered for resilience and long-term stability. A network designed to continue operating without permission or reliance on intermediaries.

Pure Proof-of-Work

Open, permissionless mining. Anyone can participate. No special roles. No privileged validators.

Unstoppable Ledger

A globally distributed ledger secured by computation, not trust. Resistant to shutdown, seizure, and manipulation.

Hard-Capped Supply

A fixed maximum supply of 30 million XTH. Enforced by consensus and not subject to change.
